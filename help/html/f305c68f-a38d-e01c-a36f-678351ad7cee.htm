RandomLaggedFibonacci Constructor (Int32, Int32, Int32) whiteMath + whiteStructs - a programmer-friendly mathematical library
Constructs a Lagged Fibonacci pseudo-random number generator with an integer seed number (uses an instance of standard Random() class for generating first sequence members) and the lag values. Examples of recommended lag values: a) 17; 5 b) 55; 24 c) 97; 33. It is not recommended to provide random lag values as it will affect the quality of the generator randomization.
